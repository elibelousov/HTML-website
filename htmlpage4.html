Research?

If one seeks to attain a level of historical accuracy when it comes to their mod, it's crucial that research is conducted to retrieve the data necessary for their changes. Please note that populations in the game are split up in states within countries, thus if someone wishes to adjust a population, they'll need to determine the population within a state rather than just within a country.
